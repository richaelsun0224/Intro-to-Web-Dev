Being minimalist doesn’t mean that you stop being hedonist.

Minimalism is about “cutting everything useless off”.
As for me, pleasures and happiness are not useless.

One of the main aims of being a minimalist is to become happy.
Our aims is based on what makes us feel happy.
So a minimalistic lifestyle can help us save time,
but make us live like a hedonist in the meantime.

Minimalist Hedonism is to guide you towards a minimal hedonistic lifestyle,
specifically in the area of cooking.
I believe that these simple but sweet,
fast but fabulous recipes will make life more beautiful.

Hope you enjoy!
