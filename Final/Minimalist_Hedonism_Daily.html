Chicken Pesto



READY IN: 1hr 10mins  |  SERVES: 8

DIRECTIONS:
1. Prepare pesto sauce according to package direction.
2. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
3. Spray rectangular dish (9 by 12 inch) with no-stick spray.
4. Moisten chicken with water, shake off excess,
and coat complete with bread crumbs.
5. Place chicken in baking dish.
6. Spread pesto sauce evenly over chicken breasts.
7. Cover each breast with ½ slice of provolone cheese.
8. Spread mushrooms over cheese.
9. Bake in oven for 45 - 50 minutes.
10. Serve with pasta.

Lemon Cheesecake




READY IN: 10mins  |  SERVES: 8

DIRECTIONS:
1. Beat cream cheese until fluffy.
2. Add sweetened condensed milk and beat again until smooth.
3. Add lemon juice, water, lemon zest, and dry pudding mix.
Beat until combined.
4. Fold in ½ of whipped topping.
5. Pour cheesecake filling into prepared pie crust.
6. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
7. Serve with whipped topping.
8. Garnish with lemon slices and chocolate curls if desired.
